# Forbes Court Reporting Shape Brief

## Sitemap

- `/` Home
- `/services` Services
- `/coverage` Coverage
- `/about` About
- `/careers-students` Careers & Students
- `/contact` Contact / Schedule

## Homepage Shape

1. Navigation with service/client path and reporter/student path.
2. Left-aligned hero with direct contact actions and a right-side record/transcript visual.
3. Proof strip: Since 1972, Western NY + Finger Lakes, Steno + Voice Reporting, Transcript Delivery.
4. Services preview with varied layout and direct path to service details.
5. Coverage preview with county/town examples.
6. Legacy section focused on family continuity and court-system experience.
7. Careers/student preview.
8. Contact CTA band.

## Service Hierarchy

Group services by proceeding type, court/public-sector work, and transcript delivery. Do not make each service its own page in v1.

## Contact Flow

The schedule form collects proceeding information and then opens a mailto draft for v1. It should feel like an intake form even without a backend.

## Mobile Behavior

Single-column, visible phone/email action, compact nav, no horizontal scroll, readable service/coverage lists.
